How can we avoid dangers on the internet?
  • Know how to report, block, and filter content: Doing this can make you stay safe in the internet when someone harrass you or someone tries to send you some content that you shouldn’t see.
  • Personalize your privacy settings: This makes you stay safe, talking only to people you know that are safe to talk.
  • Pause before you post. First ask to yourself if you don’t care about sharing the information you want to post with everyone because this content can perfectly be a safety risk.
  • Turn off geolocation: If you have geolocation activated and use a geotag to a Snapchat, update your Instagram story or you “check in” on Facebook, it means you are sharing your exact location with people you may or may not trust with it.
  • Use a private Internet connection.
  • Talk to your friends about public posts.
  • Use safe security systems on your pc: Like antivirus that can revise your pc in search of posible virus you have.
  • Report harassment or inappropriate content.
  • Know the people you talk online.
  • Look before you click.
